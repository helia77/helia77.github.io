I'm a third-year Ph.D. student with the STIM Lab at the University of Houston, where I am advised by Dr. David Mayerich. My current research focuses on creating GPU-accelerated computational methods for modeling and analyzing gigavoxel-scale data. My work is supported by the NIH Graduate Research Fellowship. I earned my B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering with a focus on Control Systems from Tehran Polytechnic University in 2021.
